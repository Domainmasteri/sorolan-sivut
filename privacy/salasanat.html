Privacy Policy for Sorolan Salasanat

Effective Date: May 2026

1. Introduction

Sorolan Salasanat ("the Extension") is a strictly local password generator committed to absolute user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Extension handles information.

2. Data Collection and Usage

The Extension is a 100% offline tool. It does not collect, store, process, or transmit any personal data, browsing history, analytics, or device information.

  • No Tracking: The Extension does not track your browsing history, monitor your behavior, or collect any analytics.
  • No Data Retention: Passwords generated by the extension are created instantaneously in your browser and are not saved or logged anywhere.

3. Required Permissions

The Extension requires the clipboardWrite permission to function. This permission is used exclusively when the user explicitly clicks the "Copy" button within the extension's popup interface to transfer the newly generated password to their clipboard.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not share, sell, or rent any data to third parties. The Extension contains no tracking codes, analytics, or advertisements, and it does not communicate with any external APIs or servers.

5. Security

The Extension runs entirely locally within your browser. Since it makes no external network requests, your generated passwords remain completely private and secure.

6. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, you can contact us at: contact.make@sorola.fi
